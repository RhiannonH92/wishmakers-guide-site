About Me

I’m not a travel agent, so I’m not here to sell you a package or pressure you into booking your holiday through me.

My only goal is to give you honest, helpful advice and the very best tips so you can get the most out of your Disney holiday.

As an independent Disney trip planner, I combine years of experience with a genuine love and passion for the parks to help you make amazing memories, enjoy a stress-free trip, and experience the magic to the fullest.

I’ve been lucky enough to have incredible Disney trips myself - riding every must-do attraction, snagging the reservations I wanted, meeting unforgettable characters and making memories I’ll never forget.

Now, it’s my turn to help YOU do the same.

My mission is to help you create a truly magical Disney holiday - one that’s stress-free, packed with joy, and full of moments you’ll treasure for a lifetime.
